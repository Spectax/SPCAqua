About Us

SPC (p) Ltd., formerly a partnership, started processing sea food as a merchant packer and managed by K. Lakshmana Swamy as managing director in 1992, with the main objective of exporting marine products nationally.

K. Lakshmana Swamy has thorough practical knowledge about the culture and processing of sea food concentrated mainly on prawns.

Quality and hygiene conscious in our processing and production catered to varied importers and quickly built a reputation for a class product and satisfied customers list. We can process, pack and export on specified customer oriented varieties.

The company also exports customised brands such as Vannami, Tiger Harvest as per customer requirements.

The managing director of the company is K. Lakshmana Swamy. His experience in shrimp culture, processing, production, and exports since 1992 and his excellent business acumen leads the company to higher achievements.
